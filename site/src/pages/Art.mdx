# Art Guide

- all art needs to be original!! 
- you're allowed to use existing fictional characters as long as you draw the art yourself
- download your art as .bmp files!
- use Aseprite! The build is on slack :D


### Helpful resources

- For finding good color palettes: [Lospec](https://lospec.com/palette-list)
- Tips + tricks for pixl art: [Miniboss Studio](https://blog.studiominiboss.com/pixelart)
- Tamagotchi sprites for inspo: [The Spriters Resource](https://www.spriters-resource.com/lcd_handhelds/tamagotchismart/)
